Meet the Owner

Robbie Smith

Heavy Equipment Operator & Licensed Septic Installer

Born and raised in Muhlenberg County, Robbie Smith knows this land like the back of his hand. With over 20 years of experience operating heavy equipment and a passion for quality workmanship, he founded RS Excavation in October 2022 to serve his community with honest, dependable excavation and septic services.

As a licensed septic installer in the state of Kentucky, Robbie takes pride in doing every job right the first time. Whether it's a new septic system, a driveway that needs grading, or a lot that needs clearing — he treats every property like it's his own.

When you hire RS Excavation, you're not dealing with a corporate office or middleman. You're working directly with Robbie — the man who shows up, operates the equipment, and makes sure you're satisfied before the job is done.
