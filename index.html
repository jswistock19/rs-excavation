Meet the Owner

Robbie Smith

Heavy Equipment Operator & Licensed Septic Installer

With over 20 years of heavy equipment experience, Robbie Smith founded RS Excavation in October 2022 to bring top-quality excavation and septic services to Muhlenberg County and the surrounding area. What started as a one-man operation with a passion for getting the job done right has grown into a trusted name in the community.

Robbie is a licensed Kentucky septic installer who takes pride in every project — whether it's installing a complete septic system, clearing land for a new build, or grading a driveway. He operates a Kubota KX060 mini excavator and skid steer with multiple attachments, giving him the versatility to handle jobs of all sizes.
