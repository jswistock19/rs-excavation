About Us

PBS Kidsteer

Skid Steer & Excavation Specialists — Muhlenberg County, KY

PBS Kidsteer is a locally owned and operated skid steer and excavation company serving Muhlenberg County and surrounding areas in western Kentucky. We take on the tough jobs others won't — from site prep and land clearing to driveways, drainage, and demolition — and we get it done right the first time.

Our team is fully licensed and insured, operating a fleet of skid steers with multiple attachments for maximum versatility. Whether it's a small residential yard job or a large commercial site prep, PBS Kidsteer shows up equipped and ready to work. Follow our Facebook page for the latest project photos and updates.
